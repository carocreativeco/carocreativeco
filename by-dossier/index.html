FAQ

What is Dossier and who is it built for?

Dossier is a professional design studio that builds premium, custom Electronic Press Kits (EPKs) and pitch decks for talent, models, actors, influencers, and business founders who have existing credentials but need them presented at an elite level.

Why shouldn't I just use a free online template?

While templates are a fine option for self-design, Dossier is a comprehensive service that delivers bespoke design, original narrative structuring, and complete copy polishing that templates cannot provide.

How long does the custom design process take?

The entire creation process takes between 5 to 10 business days, depending on how quickly you submit your raw materials, complete the intake questionnaire, and provide project feedback.

Can I get editable files of my EPK or Pitch Deck?

Standard delivery includes two optimized, presentation-ready PDF files: one for digital screens and one for high-quality printing. Fully editable source files can be selected as a project add-on.

Why does Dossier charge a fee for the initial discovery call?

The discovery call functions as a paid, working strategic session to map out your deliverables, establish goals, and map your information hierarchy, which acts as an efficient filter for serious inquiries.
