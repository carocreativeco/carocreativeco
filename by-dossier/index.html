Who this is for.

This service is designed for clients with existing work, credits, press, or proof who need their accomplishments presented professionally.

It works best if you provide your materials within one week of project kickoff and provide clear feedback within 48 hours.

This service is not a fit if you are looking for a template to edit yourself, require unlimited revisions, or are unable to commit to the timeline.

FAQs

Why not use a Canva template?

Templates can be useful for quick graphics, but Dossier is built for clients who need a custom presentation system. Your EPK or pitch deck is structured around your proof, positioning, and goals rather than forcing your work into a generic layout.

I have had bad experiences with designers before.

The process is intentionally structured. We clarify the narrative, define the materials needed, design from scratch, and keep revisions focused so the finished document looks polished and reads strategically.

What if I do not like the first draft?

One revision round is included. The first draft is built from the approved direction and materials provided, and the revision round is used to refine the design, copy, and flow.

Is an EPK and Pitch Deck worth the investment?

It is worth the investment when you already have proof, credits, work, or an offer that needs to be presented professionally to decision-makers, brands, partners, press, or investors.

I do not have all my materials ready.

This service works best when materials are provided within one week of kickoff. If core materials are missing, the project may need to be delayed or rescoped.

Why is there a fee for the discovery call?

The discovery call is a strategic review of your materials, goals, and fit. The fee protects project availability and ensures the call is used for serious, prepared inquiries.

How long does the process take?

Timing depends on project complexity and how quickly materials and feedback are provided. Clear materials within one week and feedback within 48 hours help keep the project moving.

Do I receive editable files?

Final PDFs are included. Editable source files are available as an add-on and must be requested during scoping.
